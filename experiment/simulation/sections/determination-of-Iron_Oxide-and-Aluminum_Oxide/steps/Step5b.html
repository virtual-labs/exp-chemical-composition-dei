Determination of Iron Oxide (Fe₂O₃) and Aluminium Oxide (Al₂O₃)

Step 5b: Preparing for Titration

About this step:

In this step, burette: Filled with standard potassium permanganate solution (KMnO₄).
Conical flask: Contains the solution of Fe²⁺ ions obtained after dissolving and reducing the Fe(OH)₃ precipitate.
This solution has Fe²⁺ in dilute H₂SO₄ medium. Sometimes Zn or SnCl₂ is used to reduce Fe³⁺ → Fe²⁺ before titration

Click on the knob to start the titration.
