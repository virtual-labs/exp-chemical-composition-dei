Step 3: Sample Transfer

About this step:

In this step, we will carefully transfer the weighed cement sample from the weighing container to a clean beaker. This transfer must be done carefully to avoid any loss of sample material.

The sample should be transferred completely using appropriate techniques such as rinsing the weighing container with a small amount of distilled water to ensure all sample particles are collected in the beaker. Proper sample transfer is crucial to maintain the accuracy of our quantitative analysis.

Click on the sample to transfer it to the beaker.
