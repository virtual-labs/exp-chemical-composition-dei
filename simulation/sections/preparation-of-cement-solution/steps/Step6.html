Step 6a: Filtering to Remove Insoluble Residue

About this step:

In this step, we will filter the heated solution to remove any insoluble residue. This filtration process is essential to separate the dissolved components from any undissolved particles that may remain after heating.

The filtration will be done using filter paper and a funnel. We will carefully pour the heated solution through the filter paper, allowing the liquid to pass through while retaining any solid particles. Any insoluble residue will be left on the filter paper and discarded.

Click on the funnel to begin filtering the solution.
