a) Preparation of Cement Solution

Section Overview:

This section covers the complete preparation of cement solution for chemical analysis. The process involves weighing the cement sample accurately, dissolving it in hydrochloric acid through controlled heating, and filtering to obtain a clear solution for subsequent analysis.
