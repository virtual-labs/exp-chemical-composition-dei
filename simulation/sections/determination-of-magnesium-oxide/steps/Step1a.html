e) Determination of Magnesium Oxide(MgO)

Step 1a: Precipitate Formation

About this step:

In this step, the remaining filtrate obtained after the removal of iron (Fe) and aluminium (Al) oxides in earlier parts of the analysis is taken for the determination of magnesium oxide (MgO).

The reaction that occurs is: Mg²⁺ + NH₄⁺ + HPO₄²⁻ → MgNH₄PO₄ ↓ (white precipitate)

This forms the white crystalline precipitate of magnesium ammonium phosphate (MgNH₄PO₄), which will later be filtered,
